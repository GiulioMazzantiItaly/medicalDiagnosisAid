Quick help

What is Diabetes Pedigree Function?

It is a numeric score already defined in the dataset. You can describe it as a family history or hereditary diabetes risk score.

What if I do not know some values?

Do not guess. This tool is only a study demo, so the best choice is to use recent medical data or ask a healthcare professional.
